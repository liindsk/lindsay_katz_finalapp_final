Star Generator

How to manipulate the star:

Slide your cursor over each value given in the panel on the right to customize the star to your liking.

Parameter Descriptions:

  • points:choose the number of points you want for your star
  • stroke:choose the stroke weight for your text and for the star outline
  • radius1 and radius2:choose the length of your points as well as how wide the middle space will be
  • strokeColor:choose what color you'd like the stroke of your star and text should be
  • fill:choose the fill color for your star
